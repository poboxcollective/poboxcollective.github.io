with PO Box

PO Box Collective welcomes proposals to use our space for programming, educational events, meetings, etc that align with our collective values of building community in Rogers Park through radical art making & programming, centering marginalized voices and confronting systems of oppression while fostering mutual growth & healing.

  • Click below to fill out a proposal.
  • Proposals are reviewed on a rolling basis - typically every 3 weeks.
  • All of our events are free to the public. We do not accept proposals that charge a fee to participants. A suggested donation is allowed, but organizers cannot require anyone to pay.
